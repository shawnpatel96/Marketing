Our mission is to legally provide medical marijuana to qualifying patients and to educate the community regarding the medical aspects of cannabis.



Our story, like your story, is still a work in progress. We are looking to engage with our patients to better understand what is working and what is not. We want to share knowledge and experience, so that together, we can help write the next chapter in the safe and effective use of medicinal cannabis.These repeated and passionate anecdotes of patients who were battling serious, and in many cases life threatening illnesses, effectively utilizing cannabis to mollify their pain, ease their symptoms or stimulate their appetite – to name but a few of the regularly described uses – cannot help but leave an impact on an attentive listener.
